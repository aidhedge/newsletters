A new policy report on FX-risk and ODA
In the end of May, a new and unique study was published by the Expert Group for Aid Studies, EBA. The report, authored by our director Númi Östlund, analyses the financial risk created by Swedish donor commitments in ODA. One conclusion is that there are significant improvements just waiting to happen. These solutions could provide more and better funding.
